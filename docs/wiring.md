# Wiring and Pinout

## I2C EEPROM
- SDA: GPIO 16
- SCL: GPIO 17
- I2C address: 0x50
- Pull-ups enabled

## LEDs
| LED    | GPIO |
|--------|------|
| Left   | 20   |
| Middle | 21   |
| Right  | 22   |

## Buttons
Internal pull-ups enabled (pressed = LOW)

| Button | GPIO |
|--------|------|
| Left   | 9    |
| Middle | 8    |
| Right  | 7    |
